Description

Instant ramen is the best meal after a long day with your annoying roomate and classes.

Ingredients

  • Dried noodles
  • Vegtable oil
  • Salt
  • Flavoring packet

Steps

  1. Boil the water, 2 cups of water to a boil in a sauce pan
  2. Add the noodles, gently add the dry noodle block in to the boiling water
  3. Cook the noodles, cook for the time indicated on the package (2-3 minutes)
  4. Add your seasoning packet, stir in the seasoning packet that came with the noodles
  5. Serve, you can either serve with the broth or drain most of the liquid then serve

üç≥Scrambled Eggs

Description

The perfect breakfast when you need something quick and tasty! If you want, you can always make enough for multiple people.

Ingredients

  • 2-3 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon of milk or water (optional for creamier or fluffier eggs)
  • 1/2 tablespoon of butter
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Whisk the eggs, crack eggs into a bowl, add the milk or water (if using), along with a pinch of salt
  2. Melt the butter, place a nonstick skillet over medium-low heat and melt the butter, wait until it starts to foam before adding the eggs
  3. Add the eggs, pour the egg mixture into the skillet. Let it sit for about 10‚Äì20 seconds without stirring to allow the edges to set
  4. Fold the eggs, with a rubber spatula, gently push the eggs from one side of the pan to the other, allowing the uncooked egg to flow underneath
  5. Remove from heat early, when the eggs are mostly cooked but still look moist and slightly underdone, remove the pan from the heat
  6. Serve, season with freshly cracked pepper and any additional salt
